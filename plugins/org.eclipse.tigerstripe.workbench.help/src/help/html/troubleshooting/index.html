Tigerstripe Troubleshooting

Similarly to usual Eclipse projects, Tigerstripe Projects are constantly audited by the Tigerstripe Auditor, which operates in the background and checks the model.

When errors or inconsistencies are found they are reported in the Problem View of Eclipse. Refer to Project Audit Troubleshooting for a detailed list of possible errors and their associated fixes.
