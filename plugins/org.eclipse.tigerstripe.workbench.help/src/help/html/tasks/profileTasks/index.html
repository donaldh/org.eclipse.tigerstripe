Tigerstripe Profiles

Tigerstripe Profiles help you to configure your Tigerstripe Perspective and allows you to enable or disable information from displaying on the Tigerstripe palette. The information that you disable may be information that is not utilized within your organization. Complete the following procedure to create a new Tigerstripe Profile.

To create a new Tigerstripe Profile:

  1. Click New and select Other (alternatively you can right-click on your project in the Tigerstripe Explorer, click New and select Other.) The New: Select a Wizard dialog box opens.

  2. Select Workbench Profile and click Next.
  3. Accept the default name for your profile and click Next. Your Tigerstripe Workbench Profile appears in the Tigerstripe Explorer with your default name and an extension of .wbp
