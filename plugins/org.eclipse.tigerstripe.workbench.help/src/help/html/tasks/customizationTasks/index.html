Tigerstripe Workbench Customization

Beyond the detailed options to control the generated code produced by Tigerstripe Workbench on a Service Contract model, several customization options are available to further control the output of your Tigerstripe project.

In particular, there are two areas of possible customization:

  • Customization of the Generated Integration Profiles: allows for changes in your generated code.
  • Customization of the Publish Website: allows for re-branding of websites to where your Service Contract is published.
