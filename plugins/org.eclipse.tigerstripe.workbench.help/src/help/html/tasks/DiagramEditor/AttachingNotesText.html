Attaching Notes and Text

Using the options available from the Note tool, you can attach text and notes to your diagram to further explain your service contract.

To attach a note:

  1. Click Note ( ), in the Class Diagram Editor Drawing pallet.
  2. Click in the Class Diagram Editor and the Note appears.
  3. Enter the necessary information in your note and click Save to save your changes.

To attach text:

  1. Select the drop-down menu next to the Note tool.
  2. Click Text and click in the Class Diagram Editor. An empty text box appears.
  3. Enter your text in the text box and click Save to save your changes.
